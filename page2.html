Types of Trees

Some of the trees mentioned in the table aren't specific. For example, the beech tree isn't one specific tree, it's a type that includes many other trees that classify as a beech. There's American, Coppper, Weeping, and others.

Some TYPES of trees are birch, oak, cedar, palm, and many more. Some are also mentioned in the table. The tree on the right is a palm tree. Some palm trees are Bismarck Palm (Bismarckia nobilis), Chinese Fan Palm (Livistona chinensi), Christmas Palm (Veitchia merillii), Coconut Palm (Cocos nucifera), Edible Date Palm (Phoenix dactilifera), and Royal Palm (Roystonea regia).

There are so many types of trees in the world.
