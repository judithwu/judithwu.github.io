Trees.

A tree is defined as a plant that has a permanent woody main stem, trunk, that grows to a considerable height, and usually develops branches at some distance from the ground.

There are many types of trees, and they come in many different shapes and sizes. Trees help us in many ways, and we should all appreciate trees.

This website will teach you tree facts and make you love trees.

Trees and the Environment

Trees create an ecosystem to provide habitat and food for birds and other animals.

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and potentially harmful gasses, such as sulfur dioxide, carbon monoxide, from the air and release oxygen.
